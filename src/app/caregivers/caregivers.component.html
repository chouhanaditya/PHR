Caregivers are the individuals which help patients with the basic life functions, manage medical care, help people maintain health and remain in their home or community.

Caregivers help navigate health care system, facilitate communication with health care providers, help with complex decision-making.

92-99% of caretakers are family members.


Potential Benefits of Information Sharing-


  • Enhance communication between caregiver and care provider.

  • Enhance patient engagement.

  • Improve health outcomes.

  • Decrease health care costs.
